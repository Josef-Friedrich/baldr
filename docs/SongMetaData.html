Class: SongMetaData

SongMetaData(folder)

Metadata of a song catched from the info.yml file.

arranger: Josef Friedrich
artist: Fools Garden
composer: Heinz Müller / Manfred Meier
country: Deutschland
genre: Spiritual
lyricist: Goethe

source: http://wikifonia.org/node/9928/revisions/13488/view
subtitle: A very good song
title: Lemon tree
year: 1965
