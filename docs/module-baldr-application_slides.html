(static) getInstantSlide(masterName, rawSlideData, document, config, masters) → {module:baldr-application/slides~Slide}

Show a master slide without custom data.

The displayed master slide is not part of the acutal presentation. Not every master slide can be shown with this function. It muss be possible to render the master slide without custom data. No number is assigned to the master slide.
